Putting Xdrip into Pairing Mode

  1. First go into settings - Glucose Meters and select Scan for Bluetooth Meter

  2. Now you need to pair it to your Contour Next One Meter, but before you do this.
  3. Press down for 7 sec on your Contour next meter button, to put it into pairing mode.

    And select scan. You might not need to do this if you are quick enough. Just select your Contour if it pops up

  4. Make sure to put it very close to the Note 10+ I found it better placing it at the back of the mobile!
    Select Pair. Then after you have paired it.

  5. You should now be able to turn on the option Use Bluetooth Meter by going back.

Note!
You should now be fully able to test your blood readings and have them automatically go straight into xdrip for you.


6. In the the video above I will also show you how it will get your blood readings results stright into xdrip auomaticly but for you now

Congratulations Contour Meter is now setup!
