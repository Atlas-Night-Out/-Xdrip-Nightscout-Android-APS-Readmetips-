Xdrip connection Problem
⌁



1. Make sure you are up to date on xdrip. You can do this by Following the links below
⌁

a. If you are working on a PC Download latest version of Xdrip from here Click Here or goto https://github.com/NightscoutFoundation/xDrip/releases
⌁


b. If you are working on a Mobile - Go to Chrome on your mobile & Download the latest version of Xdrip on to your Android Mobile Device Click Here to download or go to https://jamorham.github.io/#xdrip-plus in your broswer.⌁


2. Try un-pairing your Bluetooth on mobile if it’s still paired to it.You will have to wait the 2 hour warm up time again doing this process.
⌁


a. Then go to your system status in xdrip and forget device and restart the Collector or you can try with just restarting collector first to avoid the two hour warm up but does not always work this way!
⌁


b. Now Re-boot your mobile
⌁

c. After re-booting, go back to your System Status page and swipe left to your Classic Status Page and wait for it to re-pair to your Bluetooth mobile again.
⌁


This can take a while be patient, but stay on the Classic page for it to do the process of connecting to your device.
You may now need to start your sensor again and do the 2 hour warm up all depends which method you did in xdrip with forget device and restart the Collector options.
Best to re-boot again before your restart Sensor

Hope you find this video helpful and fixes your connection issues.
